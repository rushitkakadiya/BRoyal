Frequently Asked Questions

What is the payment process for my order?

To confirm your order, a 50% advance payment is required. Once we receive your advance, we will provide frequent updates with videos and images of the production process. When the final product is ready, we will send you a final video or image. The remaining 50% of the payment is due before we proceed with the delivery of your order. If you send us an item for repair, please note that shipping and other associated charges will be paid by the customer.

Can I return or Cancel my Order?

Unfortunately, once an order is placed, it cannot be returned or canceled. We encourage you to review all details carefully before completing your purchase. If you have any questions or need assistance before placing your order, our customer service team is here to help.

In how many karats of gold do you make your jewelry?

We offer a variety of karat options for our jewelry, including 10K, 14K, and 18K gold. Each karat provides a different level of purity and durability. If you have a specific karat requirement or prefer a karat not listed, please contact us.

In which types of diamonds do you make your jewelry?

We offer jewelry made with various types of diamonds, including real diamonds, lab-grown diamonds, CVD diamonds, and Moissanite. Our collection also features Silver CVD, Silver Mozo, and solitaire pieces. Our diamonds are sourced from the renowned diamond cities of Surat and Mumbai, ensuring high quality and authenticity. If you have a specific diamond type in mind, please let us know.
